Who We Are

LaVeritah Foundation is a South African non-profit organisation focused on empowering youth and women from underserved communities.

We offer life-changing programs in career guidance, subject selection, mental wellness, work readiness, and entrepreneurship development.

Beyond skills development, we aim to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity by offering mentorship, leadership training, and access to networks that can open doors for young people and women. Our programs are designed to equip participants with practical tools to navigate academic choices, career paths, and personal challenges, fostering both personal growth and community impact.

At LaVeritah Foundation, we are committed to creating spaces where hope is restored, talents are nurtured, and sustainable change is possible.
